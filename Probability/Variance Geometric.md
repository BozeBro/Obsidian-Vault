$$Var[X]=\frac{1-p}{p^2}$$
$$
\begin{align}
	\text{Var }\left[ X \right] & = \mathbb{E}\left[ X^{2} \right] -\mathbb{E}\left[ X \right] ^{2} \\
	& \frac{2-p}{p^{2}}-\left( \frac{1}{p} \right)^{2} \\
	& \frac{1-p}{p^{2}}
\end{align}
$$
