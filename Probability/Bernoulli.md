
Given X is a  [[Random Variable]], and we flip a coin. We land heads with probability p. We care about landing this head.
## [[Expectation]]
$$\mathbb{E}[X] = p$$
## [[p.m.f]]
$$p(i)=p\text{ if i is 1 else }0$$
[[c.d.f]] and [[tail]] aren't worth talking about here
