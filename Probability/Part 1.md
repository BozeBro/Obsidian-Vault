### Basic series
$$
\begin{align}
	S & = 1 + x + x^{2}+\dots + x^{n} \\
	& = \frac{1-x^{n+1}}{1-x}\\ \\
	S & = \sum_{i=0}^{\infty}x^{i} = \frac{1}{1-x} \\
\end{align}
	
$$
